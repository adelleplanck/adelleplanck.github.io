About Us

Blooming Ballerinas Studio offers high-quality ballet training in a supportive and nurturing environment.

Under the guidance of Ronè Planck, our classes focus on developing strong technique, discipline, confidence, and a genuine love for dance. We are dedicated to helping each student grow gracefully while building a solid foundation in classical ballet.

With a deep passion for ballet, she strives to share that same love with her dancers, encouraging each student to connect with the art form and develop a lasting appreciation for ballet.

More Than Just Dance

Our studio offers a variety of different classes, including ballet, hip-hop, and stretch and conditioning, ensuring that every child can find a style they love while developing well-rounded skills while having fun! The benefits of dance reach far beyond the studio, equipping children with the skills, mindset, and self-belief needed to flourish in all aspects of life.

BALLET

Provide your child a foundation for lifelong growth and confidence. They will learn new movements and techniques and refine their strength, flexibility, coordination, and posture, and gain a sense of control and awareness in their movement.

HIP-HOP

Join a dynamic and energetic studio that allows your child to explore rhythm, musicality, and self-expression in a vibrant and contemporary style. Through these classes, they build confidence, coordination, and a strong sense of individuality.

STRETCH & CONDITIONING

Further support your child’s journey by improving their strength, flexibility, and overall fitness. These sessions help prevent injury, enhance your child’s performance, and develop the discipline and resilience needed to grow in any dance style.

Join Our Family
